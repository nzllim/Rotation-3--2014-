EdgeR vs DESeq

While testing for genes that have differential expression, I have also tried alternative pipelines to see the robustness of the model used in analysis. The testing conditions are as such:

  1. Data: WT vs sxy at time = 30
  2. Q-value (Benjamini-Hochberg corrected P-value) < 0.05
  3. Models: EdgeR, DESeq
load("edger.30.RDAT")
load("deseq.30.RDAT")

# Plotting Venn Diagram to compare the two
library(VennDiagram)
venn.combine <- list(EdgeR = edger.30.data$ID, DESeq = deseq.30.data$ID)
plot.new()
venn.plot <- venn.diagram(venn.combine, filename = NULL, fill = c("red", "blue"))
grid.draw(venn.plot)

From the Venn Diagram, we can see that all the genes identified by DESeq is also found by EdgeR, however EdgeR has found more hits.

So, what are the genes that are not found by DESeq? (i.e. EdgeR-exclusive)
