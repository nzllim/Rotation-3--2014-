Progress-In-Brief (Questions Asked Thus Far)

1.What are the differentially expressed genes between wild type and sxy mutants at time = 30?

Question addressed using 2 different analysis packages (EdgeR and DESeq).
For EdgeR:
The statistical cut-off used was q-value < 0.05, where q-value is the Benjamini-Hochberg corrected p-value.
-131 genes were differentially expressed.
-77 of them were down-regulated.
-54 of them were up-regulated.

-26 of 27 known crp-S related genes were down-regulated.
-1 of 27 (ssb) did not show significant expression difference.

For DESeq:
Similar to the EdgeR pipeline, the statistical cut-off used was q-value < 0.05.
-64 genes were differentially expressed.
-Unknown genes were down or up-regulated. [To be confirmed]

-25 of 27 known crp-S related genes were differentially expressed.
They are expected to be down-regulated. [To be confirmed]
-2 out of 27 did not show significant expression difference. [To be confirmed]

2.What are the differentially expressed genes between wild type and sxy mutants at time = 0?

Question addressed using EdgeR only.
The statistical cut-off used was q-value < 0.05, where q-value is the Benjamini-Hochberg corrected p-value.
-53 genes were differentially expressed.
-Unknown genes were down or up-regulated. [To be confirmed]

-1 of 27 known crp-S related genes were differentially expressed.
-The gene is sxy (a.k.a. tfoX), and since sxy is absent in the sxy mutant, it is no surprise that it is differentially expressed.
-26 out of 27 did not show significant expression difference.
